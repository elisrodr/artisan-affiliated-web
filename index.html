How It Works

How KAE fits your
nightly order run.

KAE sits between your prep list and your vendors. You keep buying from the same people — KAE just handles the back-and-forth.

01
Share your prep list
Your closing manager types or pastes the night's order — plain English, however they think about it. "Need 3 cases romaine, 40 lbs chicken thigh, and the usual paper goods."
02
KAE sorts and routes
Every item gets matched to the right vendor using your order guides. Anything unclear, KAE asks before sending — no guessing, no surprises.
03
Orders go out
Professional order emails are sent to each vendor simultaneously — with your restaurant name, line items, quantities, and delivery date. Exactly how your reps expect them.
04
Confirmations in one place
As vendors confirm, KAE updates your dashboard. You see what's confirmed, what's pending, and what needs attention — without digging through emails.
About

Built by operators,
for operators.

"We're building the infrastructure the independent food supply chain has needed for decades — and we're starting in the field, not in a pitch deck."

Artisan Affiliated is a small founding team with an operator's mindset — built from years inside restaurant operations, supply chain logistics, and the daily grind of managing vendors, invoices, and delivery windows. We know this world because we've worked in it.

We chose Chester County and Greater Philadelphia as our testbed because depth matters more than breadth. Real restaurants, real vendors, real ordering data — that's how you build tools that actually work. KAE is the first layer: a coordination engine for ordering. Underneath, we're building a data foundation for transparency, pricing intelligence, and better decisions across the food system.
